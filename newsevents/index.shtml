Events

In the Fall and Spring semesters, CAGS hosts a visiting lecture series aimed at boosting knowledge and discussion of selected topics in Security Studies. In addition to CAGS-hosted events, CAGS also co-sponsors lectures with student groups and with other Indiana University departments and centers. You can always find an updated list of our events on our calendar. You can also see what symposia CAGS has planned in order to produce targeted research on select security related topics.
