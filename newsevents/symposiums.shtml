Symposia

CAGS periodically sponsors symposia in an effort to bring together thought leaders and scholars around particular issues. These events are not open to the public and are designed to spark discourse amongst scholars, with the goal of producing directed research. Past and upcoming symposia include:

May 15-16, 2015 - Responsibility to Protect

March 14-15, 2014 - Nuclear Stability in South Asia *With support from the School of Global and International Studies (SGIS), the Office of the Vice President for International Affairs (OVPIA), the School of Public and Environmental Affiars (SPEA), and the Carnegie Corporation of New York.

November 1-2, 2013 - Afghanistan after the 2014 US Drawdown *With support from the Center for the Study of the Middle East (CSME), School of Public and Environmental Affairs (SPEA), and the Carnegie Corporation of New York.

October 14-15, 2013 - Indian Foreign Policy Symposium *With support from the School of Public and Environmental Affairs (SPEA).

Please stay tuned to our news and events section for information on research that was published as a product of CAGS symposia.
