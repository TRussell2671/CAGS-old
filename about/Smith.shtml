Lauren Smith

Lauren Smith is an intern for the Center on American and Global Security. Before joining CAGS, Lauren was a research assistant for the Center for Research on Learning and Technology at the School of Education.  Her responsibilities include writing the weekly Congressional security bulletin, updating and maintaining the CAGS website, and aiding in student outreach efforts.

Lauren is pursuing a B.A. in Sociology and Near Eastern Languages and Cultures, with an emphasis on Arab culture and minors in Psychology and Social Welfare Advocacy. She is interested in micro-mobilization in emerging Middle Eastern democracies as well as the complexities of humanitarian intervention.  Coursework on international security at Oxford piqued her interest in security-related issues.
