Rebecca Dash

Rebecca Dash is the Associate Director of the Center on American and Global Security. Rebecca oversees the Center's day-to-day operations, budgeting and finance, partnerships with other centers and external entities, and fundraising opportunities for CAGS. Rebecca joined CAGS in October 2013 and is excited to be part of a new center within the new School of Global and International Studies.

Rebecca has a B.A. in Mathematics and Foreign Languages and Literature from Kenyon College, an Ed.M. in International Education Policy from Harvard University's Graduate School of Education, and an M.A. in Strategic Studies and International Economics from The Johns Hopkins University School of Advanced International Studies (SAIS).
