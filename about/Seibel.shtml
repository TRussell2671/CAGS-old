Jacob Seibel

Jacob is the Accounting Representative at the Center on American and Global Security where he schedules travel itineraries for guest speakers and visiting professionals. In addition, he submits payments for honorariums and reimbursements for travel expenses and monitors any issues that arise.

Prior to joining the Center, Jacob was a Plant Accountant for Poet Biorefining in Cloverdale, Indiana where he took an active role in handling the plant’s financial activities. He has also worked as a Travel Accountant for Gant Travel, Inc. in Bloomington, Indiana where he gained valuable knowledge regarding commercial travel. He holds a Bachelor’s Degree with a dual concentration in Accounting and Finance from the Kelley School of Business at Indiana University.
